ABOUT

The glowing light of fireflies in pitch black is very fantastic. The power emitted from such a small body is indescribable. The unique wetland space where fireflies live has a unique view of the world separated from everyday life. I can say that the same world is spreading to me.

Designing for me means growing myself. Coming from a small country called Japan to the United States, I wander into completely unknown territory out of curiosity about creativity. When it comes to design, a completely inexperienced person grows himself while learning design in a new frontier. The new design is the new me. Even if it is a design for someone, it leads to "for me".

The various emotions that arise in daily life are jumbled together, and sometimes light shines into the world of darkness that has fallen silent at times. My designs also have something to emphasize in their simplicity.
